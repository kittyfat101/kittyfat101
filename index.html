SVCB

Silicon Valley Computer Builders is a non-profit organization seeking to bridge the digital divide by making the internet available and usable for individuals and families in need. SVCB

  • identifies needy families and individuals
  • builds and donates computers

    and

  • provides computer and internet training

so that they can become more viable candidates in the marketplace and more productive members of society in general.

Read more about the Digital Divide to better understand the issue, and why the Silicon Valley needs the SCVB.
